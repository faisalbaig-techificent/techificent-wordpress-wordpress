Revolutionizing athlete management with WEVOLV

Industry

Sports, Athletics

Services we provided

UI/UX Design, App Development

Share Visit Website

Introduction

WEVOLV is the athlete's super app, founded by professional athletes, with the primary goal of revolutionizing career management in the sports industry. The app aims to provide a strong network for athletes, empowering them with valuable resources and connections, and assist retired athletes in finding work opportunities or advertising their profiles.

Building a solution: The birth of WEVOLV

The inception of WEVOLV stemmed from the founder Davis's personal experience and motivation to create a platform that addresses the challenges athletes face in navigating the sports industry.

Davis envisioned a platform centered around transparency, community, and support, providing athletes with the tools they need to succeed in their careers.

Features

WEVOLV offers a comprehensive suite of features tailored to athletes

Contract Analysis

Athletes can analyze their contracts and negotiate terms with confidence.

Community Connection

The app provides a platform for athletes to connect with peers, mentors, and industry professionals.

Vetted Service Providers

Athletes have access to a curated list of service providers, including agents, financial advisors, and legal experts.

Anonymous Q&A

A feature that allows athletes to seek advice and share insights anonymously within the community. The app has experienced significant growth, boasting a user base of 106 athletes, with membership being invite-only to maintain exclusivity and quality.

Challenges we faced during the project

  • Security
  • Data Management
  • Design

Challenge

Handling sensitive personal and performance data required robust security measures to protect athletes' privacy and comply with data protection regulations like GDPR.

Solution

  • Implemented end-to-end encryption for data storage and transmission.
  • Used multi-factor authentication (MFA) for user access to ensure only authorized individuals could view sensitive information.
  • Conducted regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and mitigate potential risks.
